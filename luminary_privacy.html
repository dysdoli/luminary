Luminary

Privacy Policy · Last updated 2025 · MPopTop

We don't want your data. We're not collecting it.

Luminary is a daily wisdom app developed by MPopTop. This privacy policy explains what information we collect — which is essentially nothing — and how we handle it.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. This includes your name, email address, phone number, location, device identifiers, or any other personally identifiable information.

We have no servers storing your questions, responses, or usage patterns. There are no user accounts. No sign-in is required.

What Stays On Your Device

The only data stored is a temporary local record indicating that you have received today's offering. This is saved directly on your device and is never transmitted anywhere. It clears automatically each day at midnight.

No Ads

Luminary contains no advertising of any kind. No third-party ad networks are used. No data is shared with advertisers.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for audiences 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Entertainment Purposes Only

Luminary is provided for entertainment purposes only. The wisdom passages presented in the app are drawn from various spiritual and philosophical traditions and are not intended as professional, medical, legal, or psychological advice.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
